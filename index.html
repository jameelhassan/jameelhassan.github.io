Jameel Hassan

I also work in the COVID research group at University of Peradeniya. I focus on research related to two domains in my research assistantship; computer vision and smart grids. I spend my leisure time reading books, writing (Medium) and doing Fretwork. See my works here. I enjoy playing both outdoor sports like football and basketball, and board games.

Research

I'm passionate about the intersection of computer vision and autonomous systems. Currently I am working on a cross domain research of computer vision and epidemiology to identify contacts and generate a contact heat map using CCTV footage only. I also focus on research related to Smart Grids (with Umar Marikkar ) in which I am solving an optimisation problem to utilise active and reactive power from the PV inverter to mitigate voltage violations in the Low Voltage electricity grid.

I am working on the design of a human-human interaction detection system to identify human interactions and provide a threat level assessment for a given setting in the context of COVID-19.
