Research

I'm passionate about the intersection of computer vision and autonomous systems. Currently I am working on a cross domain research of computer vision and epidemiology to identify social distancing violations such as proximity, interactions and group behaviour. I also focus on research related to Smart Grids in which I am solving an optimization problem to utilise active and reactive power from the PV inverter to mitigate voltage violations due to PV penetration in the Low Voltage electricity grid.

I am working on the design of a human-human interaction detection system to identify human interactions and provide a threat level assessment for a given location/environement, in the context of COVID-19. A deep learning approach is undertaken to initially identify and localise handshake interactions amongst people. This information is then abstracted into a graph and then processed to measure the threat level of the given scene and/or video.
