I am a PhD student at Johns Hopkins University, Maryland, USA, supervised by Vishal Patel]. . My research focus is at the intersection of machine learning and computer vision, particularly focusing on improving lidar perception using image foundation models and self-supervised representation learning techniques.

I completed my Msc in Computer Vision, [MBZUAI](https://mbzuai.ac.ae/), UAE, supervised by Salman Khan and Fahad Khan, mentored by Muzammal Naseer. I obtained my BSc in Electrical and Electronics Engineering, supervised by Prof. Janaka Ekanayake, Prof. Roshan Godaliyadda and Prof. Parakrama Ekanayake .

I like writing, find my medium blog [here](https://jameel-hassan.medium.com) and check out my hobby crafts [here](https://jameelhassan.github.io/hobbies/).
